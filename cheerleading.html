Cheer Squad Divisions

Every division is designed around age-appropriate skill-building, so cheerleaders grow at the right pace, both on the sideline and in competition.

6U

Ages 5–6

Cheerleaders learn basic motions, chants, and jumps in a fun, high-energy environment that builds confidence and team spirit.

Game Day Cheer Motions & Chants Fun First
8U

Ages 7–8

Cheerleaders build on the basics with longer routines, introductory stunts, and beginning tumbling skills.

Game Day Cheer Intro Stunts Tumbling Basics
10U

Ages 9–10

Cheerleaders take on more advanced routines with group stunts and growing tumbling skills.

Routines Group Stunts Tumbling
12U

Ages 11–12

Increased routine complexity with advanced stunts, jumps, and leadership opportunities on the squad.

Advanced Stunts Leadership Routines
14U

Ages 13–14

Our most advanced squad, performing competition-level routines with advanced tumbling and stunting.

Competition Routines Advanced Tumbling Game Ready

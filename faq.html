FAQ · Odessa, FL

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about registration, fees, schedules, and more. Can't find what you're looking for? Email us and we'll help out.

What is included in the $250 registration fee?

The $250 registration fee covers different gear and opportunities depending on the program:

Football
  • 10 regular season games
  • 2 custom uniforms (home and away)
  • Warm-up outfit
  • Custom duffel bag
  • Helmet and pad rental
Cheerleading
  • Sideline cheer at football games
  • 4+ cheer competitions
  • Sideline and competition uniforms
  • Practice outfit
  • Bows, poms, and shoes

What age groups do you offer?

We welcome athletes ages 3 through 14 across both programs:

Football

Mascots (4–6, free), 6U (5–6), 8U (7–8), 10U (9–10), 12U (11–12), and 14U (13–14). See the full breakdown on our Football Programs page.

Cheerleading

Little Legends (3–5, free), Tiny Thunder (6–7), Sparkling Hurricanes (8–9), Dynamite Divas (10–11), and Fierce Heat (12–14). See the full breakdown on our Cheer Squad Divisions page.

Do I need prior experience to join?

Not at all! Whether your athlete has never played football or cheered before, or has years of experience, there's a place for them here. Our coaches focus on fundamentals first, so everyone starts on equal footing.

When and where are practices held?

Football practices are held Mondays, Tuesdays, and Thursdays from 6:30–8:30 PM at the Keystone Park & Recreation Center located at 17928 Gunn Hwy, Odessa, FL 33556. Cheer practice schedules will be shared directly with registered families.

Do you offer sibling or family discounts?

Yes! We believe football and cheer should be accessible for the whole family—ask us about discounts when you register multiple children from the same household.

Are you hiring coaches?

Yes! We're hiring head and assistant coaches for both football and cheer. Call or text 813-475-0071 if you're interested.

How do I sign up?

Register online at our Online Registration Form. Once registered, a member of our team will follow up with next steps.
